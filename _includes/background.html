Summary

Welcome to my corner of the web. I'm currently a Senior Data Scientist at YouView, where I lead our data insights, experimentation and business intelligence team. I have a degree in data science, with a focus on urban informatics, from New York University and a background in philosophy and experimental psychology, which I read at the University of Oxford.

Data is now everywhere—wittingly or unwittingly for us old humans—and I'm interested in leveraging this ubiquity of data to design products and systems that enhance people's lives.

How do people move in a city? How do we amplify voices equitably in organizations? What is the future of learning? How can customer interactions help design products that people love? These are some of the questions I seek to answer—one dataset at a time.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably on a hammock in East London, scrolling on a smaller screen.
